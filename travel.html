Travel

If you're staying locally in Williamsburg, you might just want to go for a nice walk! For most others, we'd recommend taking the subway or getting an Uber/Lyft.

Ubers are relatively inexpensive to travel between Brooklyn, Queens, or Manhattan. We recommend grabbing a rideshare car a little early just to make sure you don't get caught in any unexpected NY traffic.

If you're driving directly to the venue, street parking is free on the surrounding blocks on weekends. Just make sure you don't park in a "no standing zone" (indicated by red signs) or in front of a garage!

If taking the subway (the MTA), the closest stops are the Grand or Graham Street L stations. Curious about public transit but not totally sure what to do? Feel free to reach out to us and we can help navigate from your hotel to the venue!
